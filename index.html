LLM-Picbreeder

Collaborative Prompt Evolution for Large Language Models

1 What is LLM-Picbreeder?

LLM-Picbreeder applies the collaborative evolutionary concepts from Picbreeder (which evolved images) to evolve prompts for Large Language Models like GPT-4.

We Evolve Prompts & Stories

Just like Picbreeder allowed communities to collaboratively evolve interesting images, LLM-Picbreeder enables communities to collaboratively evolve effective prompts that generate better content from LLMs.

2 Starting Point

We begin with a simple base prompt and let the community evolve it into more effective versions.

Initial Prompt
"Write a creative short story"
System Generates Variations

1. "Write a creative short story with a surprise ending"

2. "Write a creative short story in a fantasy setting"

3. "Write a creative short story from a child's perspective"

4. "Write a creative short story about overcoming fear"

5. "Write a creative short story with dialogue"

5 Evolutionary Breeding

The system uses top-rated prompts to create the next generation:

Crossover Operation

Parent 1: "Write a creative short story with a surprise ending"

Parent 2: "Write a creative short story from a child's perspective"

Child: "Write a creative short story from a child's perspective with a surprise ending"

Mutation Operation

Original: "Write a creative short story about overcoming fear"

Mutated: "Write a creative short story about overcoming fear in a haunted house setting"

6 Branching System

Users can branch from successful prompts to explore specialized directions:

Original Lineage
"Write a creative short story"
Branch A
"Write a creative short story for children aged 8-12"
Branch B
"Write a creative short story in a mystery genre"

7 Key Benefits

LLM-Picbreeder offers several advantages over traditional prompt engineering:

Community Collaboration

Leverage the collective intelligence of many users rather than individual efforts.

Overcomes User Fatigue

No single user needs to iterate endlessly - community explores in parallel.

Preserves Innovations

Good prompt variants are saved and can be built upon by others.

Systematic Exploration

Structured approach to exploring vast prompt design spaces.
